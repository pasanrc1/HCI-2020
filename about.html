ABOUT US


We have over 30 years experience of owning, repairing, restoring and running Rover P6 cars in all variants, 2000, 2200 & 3500, saloon and estate, both as everyday and show condition cars and also over 30 experience within classic car clubs. That experience forms the back bone of our specialist Rover P6 parts business 'MGBD Parts', that is in turn dedicated to supplying quality parts for all variants of the Rover P6 built between 1963-1977 as well as help and advice. We stock predominantly brand new manufactured items but also some second hand parts {SH} and also a small mix of 'NOS' (New Old Stock). We also do some refurbishment work to some parts while also offering a rechroming service and also workshop services, but if you can't see what you need please email us Please note that all Refurbished, NOS and Secondhand parts are detailed as such, and therefore anything else listed is a brand new manufactured item. Please click on the categories list on the left or use the product search facility to look through our items by description or part number. The vast majority of our parts are listed by the original Rover part numbers that were used in the Rover Parts catalogues, however if you don't find what you are looking for, please call us or drop us an email.
